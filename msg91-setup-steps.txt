# MSG91 SMS Setup Guide & Templates

## Step 1: Create an MSG91 Account
1. Go to https://msg91.com/ and sign up for an account.
2. Complete your KYC process if you haven't already.

## Step 2: Get your Auth Key
1. Log in to the MSG91 Dashboard.
2. Go to the "Developers" or "API" section.
3. Generate or copy your "Auth Key".
4. Add this to your `.env` file as:
   MSG91_AUTH_KEY=your_copied_auth_key_here

## Step 3: Create SMS Templates
1. In the MSG91 Dashboard, navigate to the "Flow" or "SMS Templates" section.
2. Create 7 new templates corresponding to the features in your app.
3. For each template, MSG91 will provide a `Template ID`. You must add these Template IDs to your `.env` file.

Below are the 7 templates you need to create. Ensure you use the exact variable names (e.g., {#name#}) when creating templates.

---

### Template 1: Welcome SMS
- **Template Name**: DND Welcome
- **Message Content**:
  Hello {#name#}, welcome to DND App! We are excited to have you on board.
- **Variable**: {#name#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_WELCOME=template_id_here

### Template 2: New Enquiry Alert (For Sellers)
- **Template Name**: DND New Enquiry
- **Message Content**:
  New Enquiry Alert! A new Enquiry matching your categories has been posted on DND. Log in to submit an offer.
- **Variables**: (None)
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_NEW_Enquiry=template_id_here

### Template 3: Bidding Started (For Sellers)
- **Template Name**: DND Bidding Started
- **Message Content**:
  Bidding has started for Enquiry #{#EnquiryId#}. Log in now to submit your offers before time runs out!
- **Variable**: {#EnquiryId#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_BIDDING_STARTED=template_id_here

### Template 4: New Offer Received (For Buyers)
- **Template Name**: DND New Offer
- **Message Content**:
  New Offer Received! You have a new offer on Enquiry #{#EnquiryId#}. Log in to review it.
- **Variable**: {#EnquiryId#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_NEW_OFFER=template_id_here

### Template 5: Offer Accepted (For Buyers)
- **Template Name**: DND Offer Accepted Buyer
- **Message Content**:
  Success! You have accepted Offer #{#offerId#}. Log in to see the seller's details.
- **Variable**: {#offerId#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_ACCEPTED_BUYER=template_id_here

### Template 6: Offer Accepted (For Sellers)
- **Template Name**: DND Offer Accepted Seller
- **Message Content**:
  Congratulations! Your Offer #{#offerId#} has been accepted. Log in to contact the buyer.
- **Variable**: {#offerId#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_ACCEPTED_SELLER=template_id_here

### Template 7: Offer Rejected (For Sellers)
- **Template Name**: DND Offer Rejected Seller
- **Message Content**:
  Update: Your Offer #{#offerId#} was not accepted this time. Keep bidding on other inquiries!
- **Variable**: {#offerId#}
- **.env Variable**: MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_REJECTED_SELLER=template_id_here

---

## Step 4: Update your .env File
Copy and paste this block into your `.env` file, replacing the placeholder values with the actual Template IDs and Auth Key from MSG91.

MSG91_AUTH_KEY=your_msg91_auth_key
MSG91_TEMPLATE_WELCOME=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_NEW_Enquiry=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_BIDDING_STARTED=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_NEW_OFFER=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_ACCEPTED_BUYER=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_ACCEPTED_SELLER=
MSG91_TEMPLATE_OFFER_REJECTED_SELLER=


## Step 5: DLT Registration (For Indian Numbers Only)
If you are sending SMS to Indian mobile numbers, you will also need to:
1. Register your business on a DLT platform (like Jio, PingConnect, trueconnect, etc.).
2. Register your "Sender ID" on DLT.
3. Register these exact message templates on the DLT platform to get Template IDs, and add these DLT Template IDs to your MSG91 templates.
